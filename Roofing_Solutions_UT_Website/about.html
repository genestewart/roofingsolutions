    Frequently Asked Questions

    Have questions? We’ve compiled a list of the most common inquiries we receive about roofing, snow removal, and snow melting systems. If you don’t see your question here, feel free to reach out to us!

    How long does a metal roof last?

    Metal roofs can last 50 years or more with proper maintenance, making them a highly durable and cost-effective option for homeowners.

    What are the benefits of a snow melting system?

    Snow melting systems help prevent ice dams, reduce the risk of roof damage, and eliminate the need for manual snow removal.

    How do I know if my roof needs replacing?

    Signs include leaks, missing shingles, sagging areas, or roofing that is over 20 years old. Our team can inspect your roof and advise you on whether replacement is necessary.
